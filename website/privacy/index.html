Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2024

Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains what information Openly collects, how we use it, and the choices you have. We will never sell your personal data.

1. Information We Collect

When you use Openly, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Account information: Your email address, username, display name, and profile photo when you create an account.
  • Content you create: Posts, replies, messages, images, and other content you submit to the platform.
  • Device information: Device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers, and mobile network information.
  • Usage data: How you interact with the app — screens viewed, features used, and actions taken (such as likes and follows).
  • Log data: IP address, access times, pages viewed, and crash reports when errors occur.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, operate, and improve the Openly service
  • Create and maintain your account
  • Display your content to other users according to your settings
  • Send important service notifications (security alerts, policy updates)
  • Detect and prevent abuse, spam, and violations of our Terms of Service
  • Analyse usage patterns to improve app performance and features
  • Respond to your support requests

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. We may share data only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service providers: Trusted third-party vendors who help us operate our infrastructure (hosting, analytics, crash reporting), bound by confidentiality agreements.
  • Legal requirements: When required by law or to protect the rights, property, or safety of Openly, our users, or the public.
  • Business transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your data may be transferred as part of that transaction.

Public content you post (e.g. public posts and your profile) is visible to other users and may be indexed by search engines.

4. Data Retention

We retain your account information and content for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will delete your personal data within 30 days, except where we are required to retain it for legal or safety reasons.

5. Security

We take security seriously and implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your information, including:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Secure password hashing
  • Access controls limiting who can view your data
  • Regular security reviews

No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Export your data in a portable format

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at support@openlypal.com.

7. Children's Privacy

Openly is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will take steps to delete it.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by posting a notice in the app or sending you an email. Your continued use of Openly after changes take effect constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

Email: support@openlypal.com
