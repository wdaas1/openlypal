Common Questions

How do I delete my account?

Open the Openly app, go to Settings → Account → Delete Account. Your data will be permanently deleted within 30 days. If you need assistance, email us at support@openlypal.com.

How do I report a user or post?

Tap the three-dot menu (⋯) on any post or profile and select "Report". Our moderation team reviews all reports. For urgent safety concerns, contact us directly by email.

I forgot my password — how do I reset it?

On the sign-in screen, tap "Forgot password?" and enter your email address. You'll receive a password reset link within a few minutes. Check your spam folder if you don't see it.

How do I request my data?

Email support@openlypal.com with the subject "Data Request" and we will send you an export of your personal data within 30 days.
